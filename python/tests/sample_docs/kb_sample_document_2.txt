FAQ Document

Q: What is a knowledge base?
A: A knowledge base is a centralized repository of information that can be easily accessed and searched.

Q: Why use a knowledge base?
A: Knowledge bases help organize information, improve accessibility, and maintain consistency in documentation.

Q: How to structure a knowledge base?
A: Common structures include:
1. Categories and subcategories
2. Tags and metadata
3. Hierarchical organization
4. Cross-referencing
